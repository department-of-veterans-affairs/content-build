# Interest Rate Reduction Refinancing Loan Worksheet (VA Form 26-8923)

## What this tool does

This digital worksheet helps VA-approved lenders calculate the maximum loan
amount for an Interest Rate Reduction Refinancing Loan (IRRRL). The tool
automatically calculates all 18 lines of the worksheet — including the
Section III iterative funding fee recalculation — and checks that fees stay
within VA regulatory limits.

When you finish, you can download a completed VA Form 26-8923 (November 2024
version) to print, sign, and include in your IRRRL closing package.

**This form is for VA-approved lenders only.** If you are a Veteran who wants
to learn more about IRRRLs, visit our
[Interest Rate Reduction Refinancing Loan page](/housing-assistance/home-loans/loan-programs/irrrl).

---

## Who completes this form

An officer of a VA-approved lending institution completes this form when
requesting VA guaranty on an IRRRL. The officer must sign the printed
worksheet in ink before including it in the closing package, unless your
institution has separate VA authorization for electronic signatures.

---

## Eligibility — when an IRRRL worksheet is required

You must complete VA Form 26-8923 when:

- You are a VA-approved lender requesting VA guaranty on an IRRRL
- The loan will refinance an existing VA-guaranteed loan
- You need to document the maximum allowable loan amount for the closing package

**Note:** An IRRRL may only refinance an existing VA-guaranteed loan. The
Veteran must occupy or have previously occupied the property as their primary
residence.

---

## What you'll need before you start

Have the following information ready before you begin. You cannot save and
return to this worksheet once you start, so gather everything first.

**Loan information:**
- VA loan number for the existing VA-guaranteed loan being refinanced
  (found on the original loan closing documents)
- Outstanding balance of the existing VA loan, from the servicer payoff
  statement
- Amount of any cash payment the Veteran is making toward the loan (enter 0
  if none)

**Fee information:**
- Discount points percentage (if any)
- Origination fee percentage — **cannot exceed 1% of the loan amount**
  per 38 CFR § 36.4313
- VA funding fee percentage — **0.5% for IRRRLs** per 38 CFR § 36.4312
  (enter 0 only if the Veteran is exempt from the funding fee)
- Total of all other allowable closing costs and prepaids (title insurance,
  recording fees, prepaid taxes, prepaid homeowners insurance, etc.)

**Lender information:**
- Full legal name of the lending institution
- Name and title of the certifying officer

---

## How long this takes

**About 10 minutes** to complete the digital worksheet. This is a significant
reduction from the estimated 30-minute manual process for the paper form
(OMB-stated respondent burden).

---

## What happens after you submit

1. The tool generates a completed VA Form 26-8923 (November 2024 version) as a PDF.
2. Print the PDF.
3. Have an officer of your lending institution sign in ink in the signature block.
4. Include the signed worksheet in your IRRRL closing package submitted through
   WebLGY or your VA Regional Loan Center.
5. Keep a copy for your institution's records.

**If any loan values change before closing** (for example, the payoff balance
changes), return to this tool, update the values, and generate a new PDF. Do
not alter the PDF manually.

If you cannot use this digital tool, you can download the PDF version of

[VA forms library](https://www.va.gov/find-forms/about-form-26-8923).

**Important:** Use only the November 2024 version of this form. Earlier
versions — including the December 2021 version — are superseded and should
not be submitted. The version number appears on the face of the form.
