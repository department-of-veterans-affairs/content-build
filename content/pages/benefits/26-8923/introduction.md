# Interest Rate Reduction Refinancing Loan Worksheet (VA Form 26-8923)

VA Form 26-8923 is the worksheet VA-approved lenders complete to calculate
the maximum loan amount for a VA Interest Rate Reduction Refinancing Loan
(IRRRL). This digital tool calculates all amounts automatically as you
enter loan data, catches common errors before they reach VA's review team,
and generates a completed November 2024 version of the form.

**OMB Control Number:** 2900-0386  
**Expiration date:** November 30, 2027  
**Estimated completion time:** 10 minutes (digital tool) — reduced from the
30-minute OMB-stated burden for the paper form

---

## Who completes this form

An officer of a VA-approved lending institution completes this worksheet.
Loan officers, processors, and closers may use this tool to prepare the
calculation, but the printed PDF must be reviewed and signed in ink by an
authorized officer of the lending institution before it is included in the
IRRRL closing package.

**This form is NOT completed by the Veteran.** Veterans who want to
understand the maximum loan amount calculation for their IRRRL can ask their
lender to share the completed worksheet.

---

## What this worksheet calculates

This worksheet computes the maximum allowable loan amount for an IRRRL
across three sections:

- **Section I (Lines 1–3):** Adjusts the existing VA loan balance by
  subtracting any cash payment the Veteran is making toward the loan.
- **Section II (Lines 4–9):** Adds allowable fees — discount points,
  origination fee, VA funding fee, and other allowable closing costs — to
  reach a preliminary loan amount.
- **Section III (Lines 10–18):** Runs the required iterative recalculation
  to arrive at the final Maximum Loan Amount (Line 18). The tool performs
  this calculation automatically. Line 18 must always be rounded down.

---

## Eligibility — when to use this form

Use this worksheet for every IRRRL where you are requesting VA loan
guaranty. You need a completed VA Form 26-8923 when:

- You are refinancing an existing VA-guaranteed loan into a new VA-guaranteed
  loan under the Interest Rate Reduction Refinancing Loan program.
- The new loan will include any financed fees (origination fee, VA funding
  fee, discount points, or other allowable closing costs).
- You are submitting an IRRRL guaranty package through WebLGY or to a VA
  Regional Loan Center.

**Do not use this form for:**

- VA purchase loans (use VA Form 26-6393)
- VA cash-out refinance loans (this worksheet applies to IRRRLs only)
- Any refinance where the existing loan is not VA-guaranteed

## What you'll need before you start

Have these documents and pieces of information ready before you begin.
The tool calculates everything else automatically.

1. **VA Loan Number** — the loan number for the existing VA-guaranteed loan
   being refinanced. This appears on the original loan closing documents.
2. **Servicer payoff statement** — the current outstanding balance of the
   existing VA loan from the mortgage servicer. You will enter this as
   Line 1. Use a current payoff statement; a stale balance is the most
   common source of errors on this worksheet.
3. **Cost of any energy efficient improvements** (if applicable) — if the
   Veteran is financing energy efficient improvements into the new loan,
   add that amount to the existing loan balance before entering Line 1.
4. **Discount points percentage** (Line 5) — expressed as a percentage of
   the loan amount. Enter 0 if no discount points apply. Discount points
   above 2% may require special justification per 38 CFR § 36.4313.
5. **Origination fee percentage** (Line 6) — the origination fee may not
   exceed 1% of the loan amount per 38 CFR § 36.4313. The tool blocks
   submission if you enter more than 1%.
6. **VA funding fee percentage** (Line 7) — pre-filled at 0.5% per
   38 CFR § 36.4312 for all IRRRLs. Enter 0 only if the Veteran is exempt
   from the funding fee. You must verify and document any exemption in the
   closing package.
7. **Other allowable closing costs and prepaids** (Line 8) — the total of
   all other allowable costs not captured in Lines 5–7, including title
   insurance, recording fees, prepaid taxes, and prepaid homeowners
   insurance. See VA Pamphlet 26-7, Chapter 8, for the complete list.
8. **Lender officer information** — the full legal name of your lending
   institution and the name and title of the officer who will certify and
   sign the worksheet.

---

## Important rules for IRRRL loan amounts

- **The maximum loan amount (Line 18) must always be rounded down** —
  never up. A loan amount above the calculated maximum will cause cash to
  be disbursed to the Veteran, which is prohibited for an IRRRL.
- **The origination fee cannot exceed 1% of the loan amount** per
  38 CFR § 36.4313. This tool enforces this cap automatically.
- **The VA funding fee for an IRRRL is 0.5%** per 38 CFR § 36.4312.
  The tool will not allow a funding fee above 0.5%.
- **Cash back to the Veteran is not permitted on an IRRRL.** Any loan
  amount that results in cash to the Veteran at closing is a prohibited
  transaction.

---

## After you complete this worksheet

After you submit the worksheet, the tool will:

1. Generate a completed PDF of VA Form 26-8923 (November 2024 version).
2. Provide a confirmation number and a record of Line 18 (Maximum Loan
   Amount).

**You must then:**

1. Download and print the generated PDF.
2. Have an authorized officer of your lending institution sign the signature
   block in ink.
3. Include the signed worksheet in your IRRRL closing package submitted
   through WebLGY or to your VA Regional Loan Center.
4. Retain a copy for your institution's records.

**Do not alter the generated PDF manually.** If any input value changes
before closing (for example, you receive a new payoff statement with a
different balance), return to this tool, update the values, and generate a
new PDF.

## Privacy Act statement

**Authority:** 38 U.S.C. 3710; 38 CFR Part 36.  
**Purpose:** VA uses the information on this form to determine the maximum
allowable loan amount for an Interest Rate Reduction Refinancing Loan and
to process the request for VA loan guaranty.  
**Routine uses:** The information may be disclosed to federal, state, and
local agencies as required by law, and to lenders and servicers involved
in the loan transaction as permitted under the Privacy Act.  
**Effect of not providing information:** VA cannot process the IRRRL
guaranty request without this worksheet.  

Respondent burden for this information collection is estimated at 10
minutes per response using the digital tool (30 minutes for the paper form),
including the time for reviewing instructions, searching data sources,
gathering and maintaining the data needed, and completing and reviewing the
collection. Send comments regarding this burden estimate or any other
aspect of this information collection to: VA Reports Clearance Officer,
810 Vermont Avenue, NW, Washington, DC 20420. Do not send completed forms
to this address.
