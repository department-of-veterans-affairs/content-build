# Get help with Enrollment Certification for Post-9/11 GI Bill

**VA Form 22-1999b**

## Common questions about this form

### Who can submit this form?

Only authorized School Certifying Officials (SCOs) can submit this form. You must be designated by your school as an SCO and registered with VA.

### When should I submit an enrollment certification?

Submit this form:
- **For new students:** Within 30 days of the term start date
- **For continuing students:** At the beginning of each new term
- **For changes:** Within 30 days of any enrollment changes
- **For withdrawals:** Within 30 days of the student's last date of attendance

### What's the difference between credit hours and training hours?

- **Credit hours:** Used for college and university degree programs
- **Training hours:** Used for non-college programs like apprenticeships, on-the-job training, or certificate programs

### How do I find my VA facility code?

Your 8-character VA facility code is assigned when your school gets approved for VA benefits. Contact your Education Liaison Representative if you don't know your code.

### What if the student is taking both online and on-campus classes?

Select "Mixed (online and on-campus)" as the training location. This affects the housing allowance calculation for the student.

### Can I certify multiple students at once?

This form is for one student at a time. For high-volume certifications, you may want to continue using VA-ONCE or contact us about bulk submission options.

### What happens if I make a mistake on the form?

You can submit a new form with the correct information. Mark it as "Enrollment change" and explain the correction in the reason field.

### Why was my certification rejected?

Common reasons include:
- Invalid or incorrect VA facility code
- Missing required information
- Dates that don't match your academic calendar
- Student not eligible for benefits

Check your email for specific rejection reasons and resubmit with corrections.

## Changes to existing certifications

### How do I report a withdrawal?

1. Select "Withdrawal" as the type of change
2. Enter the student's last date of attendance
3. Explain the reason for withdrawal
4. Submit as soon as possible to avoid overpayments

### What if a student reduces their credit hours?

Submit a new certification selecting "Enrollment change" and enter the new credit hours. This may affect their enrollment status and benefit payments.

### How do I report a leave of absence?

Select "Leave of absence" as the change type. Include the expected return date if known. Submit another certification when the student returns.

## Technical help

### I'm having trouble signing in

You need a verified VA.gov account to access this form. [Create an account or verify your identity](https://www.va.gov/resources/signing-in-to-vagov/).

### The form won't let me continue

Make sure you've filled out all required fields marked with a red asterisk (*). Check that your facility code is correct and that all dates are in the right format.

### I can't upload my documents

Supported file types: PDF, JPG, PNG. Files must be smaller than 25MB. If you're still having trouble, try a different browser or contact technical support.
