# Enrollment Certification for Post-9/11 GI Bill

**VA Form 22-1999b**

Use this form to certify enrollment for Veterans and beneficiaries using Post-9/11 GI Bill benefits at your VA-approved school or training facility.

**Who should use this form**

You should use this form if you're a School Certifying Official (SCO) and you need to:

- Certify new enrollment for a Veteran or beneficiary
- Report changes to existing enrollment
- Certify continued enrollment for a new term
- Report student withdrawals or leaves of absence

**Time to complete this form:** About 10 minutes

## What you'll need to complete this form

**School information:**
- Your VA facility code (8-character code)
- Your title as School Certifying Official
- School's official name and address

**Student information:**
- Student's full name (as it appears on military records)
- Student's Social Security number or VA file number
- Student's date of birth
- Contact information for the student

**Enrollment details:**
- Program of study or course name
- Term start and end dates
- Number of credit hours or training hours per week
- Student's enrollment status (full-time, part-time, etc.)

**Training information:**
- Type of program (degree, certificate, on-the-job training, etc.)
- Training location (on campus, online, or mixed)
- ZIP code where training takes place

**For changes or withdrawals:**
- Type of change being reported
- Effective date of the change
- Reason for withdrawal or change

## How long does this process take?

**Online submissions:** 14 to 21 days to process

**What happens after you submit**
After you submit this form, we'll review your certification. The Veteran or beneficiary will receive payment authorization within 2 to 3 weeks for complete and accurate submissions.

Students can check their payment status online at VA.gov or by calling the GI Bill Hotline at 888-442-4551.

## Other ways to submit this form

**By mail:** You can still submit paper forms, but processing takes 4 to 5 weeks longer.

**Through VA-ONCE:** If your school has access to VA-ONCE, you can continue using that system.

**Need help?**
- Call the School Certifying Official Hotline: 918-781-5678
- Email: SCO.VREO@va.gov
- [Find your Education Liaison Representative](https://www.va.gov/education/school-certifying-officials/liaison-representatives/)
